How it Started

Founded by Derek Odaro (Dotty), the Dotty Music Foundation was born in the streets of Nigeria. Seeing the immense raw talent in music, fashion, and dance being wasted due to a lack of infrastructure, Dotty decided to create a bridge.

What started as a small talent hunt has grown into a movement that provides recording deals, equipment, and professional mentorship to those who need it most.

Derek "Dotty" Odaro

President & Visionary

As a music executive, Derek knows exactly what the industry looks for. He founded this organization to ensure that "underprivileged" doesn't mean "underrepresented."

Under his leadership, the foundation has successfully hosted national talent hunts and secured life-changing deals for youth across Abuja and beyond.
