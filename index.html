ABOUT US .

We find the stars of tomorrow and give them the stage they deserve today

talent is universal, but opportunity is not. Founded by visionary music executive Derek Odaro—universally known as "Dotty"—the foundation emerged from a desire to bridge the gap between raw, natural ability and the resources required to turn that ability into a sustainable career.

Derek Odaro

President & CEO. Driven by the vision of nurturing the next generation of stars.

The Mission

To empower underprivileged Nigerian youth through accessible music education and mentorship. We leverage the universal language of music to transform lives, foster creativity, build unity across divides, and create sustainable employment opportunities that keep young people away from social vices.

Artist Support

We provide comprehensive development for the next generation of music stars—from singers and producers to dancers and content creators. Our support includes mentorship, studio access, promotional backing, and professional networking. We actively sign promising talents like Afrobeats artist Freshgee, guiding them from raw ability to sustainable careers.

Talent Hunts

Our high-profile talent shows provide a national platform for undiscovered youth. The 2023 grand finale at Abuja's Transcorp Hilton—mentored by industry icons like Shina Rambo and Joel Amadi—awarded over ₦1.8 million in prizes and professional management deals, turning dreams into tangible career pathways.

Record Label Signing

The winner of our talent showcases receives a direct signing to Derek Odaro's personal record label, providing professional recording, distribution, and career guidance this ensures long-term industry integration for our brightest stars.

Management Deals

Runners-up in our competitions secure professional music management contracts, receiving structured career development, industry navigation support, and exposure opportunities.

Equipment Support

Beyond cash prizes, we provide musical equipment to talented participants through our sponsors, ensuring they have the essential tools to continue creating and practicing their craft professionally

Derek "Dotty" Odaro
